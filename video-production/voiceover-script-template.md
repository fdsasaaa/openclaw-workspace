## 完整脚本模板

```
This is Day 1, March 7th, 2026, and I'm excited to share what happened.

Let's dive in!
```

**时长：** 约25秒

### 今日概览（0:30-1:30，1分钟）

**画面：** MT5主界面 + 数据面板

**配音：**
```
So today was a pretty interesting day in the markets.

Out of these [X] trades, [X] were winners and [X] were losers,
giving us a win rate of [X]%.

The total profit for today is [+$XXX / -$XXX].
```

**变量：**
- [X] setups

- [+$XXX / -$XXX] profit

### 交易1分析（1:30-3:30，2分钟）

**画面：** MT5图表 + 交易标注

**配音：**
```
Let's start with Trade Number 1.

This was on [SYMBOL] on the [H1/H4] timeframe.

The strategy detected a box formation between [PRICE1] and [PRICE2].

I entered at [ENTRY_PRICE] when price broke above/below the box.

My stop loss was at [SL_PRICE], and my take profit was at [TP_PRICE].

This gave me a risk-reward ratio of [1:X].

[IF WIN:]
The trade went in my favor, and I exited at [EXIT_PRICE] 
for a profit of [+X] pips, which is [+$XXX].

[IF LOSS:]
Unfortunately, the trade hit my stop loss at [SL_PRICE]
for a loss of [-X] pips, which is [-$XXX].

[LESSON:]
The key lesson here is [LESSON_TEXT].
```

**字数：** 约120词
**时长：** 约1分50秒

**变量：**
- [SYMBOL] - 交易品种
- [H1/H4] - 时间周期
- [PRICE1], [PRICE2] - 箱体价格
- [ENTRY_PRICE] - 入场价格
- [SL_PRICE] - 止损价格
- [TP_PRICE] - 止盈价格
- [1:X] - 风险回报比
- [EXIT_PRICE] - 出场价格
- [+/-X] pips - 盈亏点数
- [+/-$XXX] - 盈亏金额
- [LESSON_TEXT] - 经验教训

## 脚本生成工具

### Python 脚本模板

```python
def generate_script(trades_data):
    """
    根据交易数据生成配音脚本
    
    trades_data = {
        'date': 'March 7, 2026',
        'total_trades': 5,
        'winners': 4,
        'losers': 1,
        'win_rate': 80,
        'total_profit': 250,
        'trades': [
            {
                'number': 1,
                'symbol': 'EURUSD',
                'timeframe': 'H1',
                'entry': 1.3900,
                'exit': 1.3950,
                'pips': 50,
                'profit': 125,
                'result': 'win'
            },
            # ...
        ]
    }
    """
    
    script = f"""
    Hey traders! Welcome back to my channel.
    
    Today I'm showing you the results of my semi-automated box strategy on MT5.
    
    This is Day 1, {trades_data['date']}, and I'm excited to share what happened.
    
    Let's dive in!
    
    ---
    
    So today was a pretty interesting day in the markets.
    
    I took {trades_data['total_trades']} trades in total.
    
    Out of these {trades_data['total_trades']} trades, 
    {trades_data['winners']} were winners and {trades_data['losers']} were losers,
    giving us a win rate of {trades_data['win_rate']}%.
    
    The total profit for today is ${trades_data['total_profit']}.
    
    Now let me walk you through each trade.
    """
    
    # 添加每个交易的分析
    for trade in trades_data['trades']:
        script += f"""
        
        ---
        
        Trade Number {trade['number']} was on {trade['symbol']} 
        on the {trade['timeframe']} timeframe.
        
        I entered at {trade['entry']} and exited at {trade['exit']}
        for a {'profit' if trade['result'] == 'win' else 'loss'} 
        of {trade['pips']} pips, which is ${trade['profit']}.
        """
    
    return script
```
